About Report-It

Report-It is a civic issue reporting platform designed to empower citizens of Karnataka to easily report problems such as potholes, garbage issues, water leakage, streetlight outages, and more.

With features such as real-time status tracking, location tagging, and department-wise complaint routing, Report-It ensures that civic problems are addressed efficiently and transparently.

Government of Karnataka Initiative

This initiative is in collaboration with the Government of Karnataka to enhance the accountability and responsiveness of civic departments.

Our vision is a cleaner, safer, and better-connected society driven by active public participation and tech-enabled governance.
